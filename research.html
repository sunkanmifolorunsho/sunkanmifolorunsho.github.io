Research & Publications

Examining the social determinants of health, aging, and inequality

My research program examines how social structures, inequalities, and power relations shape health outcomes across the life course. Using mixed methods and longitudinal data analysis, I investigate the complex relationships between economic security, social support, and health among older adults in both the United States and Sub-Saharan Africa.
