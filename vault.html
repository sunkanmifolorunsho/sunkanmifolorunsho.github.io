2026

NEW PUBLICATION

Social Engagement and Cognitive Health in Older Black Adults

Published in Alzheimer's & Dementia, January 2026

This study examines how social engagement moderates the complex relationships between cognitive functioning, depressive symptoms, and restless sleep among older Black adults. Using longitudinal data, the research demonstrates that higher levels of social engagement buffer against the negative effects of cognitive decline on sleep quality and mental health.

NEW PUBLICATION

Racial and Socioeconomic Disparities in Hospice Utilization

Published in American Journal of Hospice & Palliative Care, 2026

This research investigates patterns of hospice care access and utilization among older adults in the United States, revealing persistent racial and socioeconomic disparities in end-of-life care. The findings highlight the need for policy interventions to ensure equitable access to palliative care services.

PUBLICATION

Masculinity and Mental Health in Later Life in Sub-Saharan Africa

Published in Sociology Compass, December 2025

A comprehensive review examining the intersection of masculine norms and mental health outcomes among older men in Sub-Saharan African contexts. This work synthesizes recent literature to identify critical gaps in understanding gendered experiences of aging.
